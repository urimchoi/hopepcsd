Title: College Group Pastor

Role: leader

Bio: Sun was born in Seoul and was raised in St. Paul/Minneapolis for the majority of his childhood. Having moved to Denver for his high school education and to San Diego for his college education, he has steadily moved westward in his pursuit of higher education. In sunny California, he received his B. A. in Economics at UC San Diego, and he met his beautiful wife Judy whom he has been married to since 2013. As a student at Westminster Seminary California, Sun is currently pursuing his M. Div and is set to graduate in the spring of 2015 while pursuing ordination during the same year. Having served Hope Presbyterian Church as an intern pastor since Winter 2011, he has been blessed in ministering to college students in the San Diego area growing alongside them in Christ.
